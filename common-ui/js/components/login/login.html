Welcome to ENERGY STAR® RaterPRO™, a free mobile app and web tool developed by the U.S. EPA to help home energy raters efficiently and effectively get their field work done. Raters can use RaterPRO™ to verify homes to meet ENERGY STAR® program requirements or to facilitate data collection to generate Energy Rating Index scores.

RaterPRO™ Support

Finish the Job on the Job Site!

With RaterPRO, all required paperwork is completed before the rater leaves the inspection:

The ENERGY STAR® Rater Field Checklist is filled out.

Required updates to construction specs are synced back to the office in real-time.

An action report is sent to the builder noting any needed corrections to be made.

Photos & notes are included in the digital job file, no need to clutter phone camera roll or office files.

Rather than relying on paper checklists and hand-written notes, RaterPRO allows home energy raters to electronically complete required on−site inspections, document diagnostic testing results, capture digital photos and notes, and geotag the inspection location.

This all-digital workflow helps rating companies large and small deliver accurate, high-quality ratings and ENERGY STAR certifications. Best of all, there’s no more tedious data entry thanks to two-way import/export with energy modeling software that eliminates transcription errors and saves time on every job.

Built for the Field

From the start, RaterPRO™ was designed for the rigors of the jobsite. The native mobile app features a nimble, friendly interface that’s easy to use in the field.

Remote job site? No problem. RaterPRO™ works with or without internet access and automatically syncs to the cloud as soon as it reconnects.

Demonstrate Quality

RaterPRO™ is a tool for doing high-quality ratings, allowing energy rating companies and oversight organizations to authenticate the quality of every rating with a new level of assurance. A required front elevation photo, inspection location tracking, and detailed Job History report provide a full accounting of each job.
