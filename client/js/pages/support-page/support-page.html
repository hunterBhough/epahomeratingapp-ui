Getting Started with RaterPRO

RaterPRO is a free app for collecting high quality field data during ENERGY STAR and standalone HERS rating inspections. Developed by the US EPA’s ENERGY STAR New Homes team, RaterPRO is freely available for use by all energy rating companies.

Interested in joining ENERGY STAR? Learn more here.

Interested in using RaterPRO at your company? Learn how to get started below:

Mobile Tablets

RaterPRO supports iPad running iOS version 10.0 and higher. Check which version of iOS your machine is running by going to Settings > General > About.

RaterPRO is not currently available in other platforms.

Rating Software

RaterPRO supports file exchange with the latest versions of REM/Rate, Ekotrope, and EnergyGauge (v 5.2.00 or greater) software.

Note that ‘Threshold Ratings’ are not supported at this time.

RaterPRO is available to Energy Rating Companies, regardless of their affiliation with the ENERGY STAR New Homes Program. See below for details on getting started as either an existing ENERGY STAR Partner or a HERS Only Non-ENERGY STAR Partner:

ENERGY STAR Energy Rating Company

Existing ENERGY STAR Partners start by checking that their organization’s My ENERGY STAR Account (MESA) is up to date. All RaterPRO users (admin, rater and provider users) must be listed as contacts in the organization’s MESA account.

Rater users, specifically, should have following information listed in their MESA contacts:

  • Rater ID Number – Raters accounts must include a valid Rater ID number to perform HERS and ENERGY STAR ratings
  • V3 Eligible – Though all Raters may perform standalone HERS inspections, raters conducting ENERGY STAR ratings must have completed ENERGY STAR Version 3 Training.

Contact us at energystarhomes@energystar.gov for help updating your organization’s MESA accounts.

Once all contact information is up-to-date and all mandatory Rater fields are complete, your organization is ready to be invited to RaterPRO. Contact us at energystarhomes@energystar.gov and indicate your organization’s primary RaterPRO contact person to request access.

Note: RaterPRO is a separate platform with different login credentials from your existing My ENERGY STAR Account (MESA). You will be given the opportunity to create new login credentials for RaterPRO in an invitation email.

Follow the steps below to create a User ID and password, download the RaterPRO mobile app, read the User Guide, and familiarize yourself with the system.

When you are ready, you may invite additional contacts to RaterPRO on the Users tab of the desktop site. Please review ‘Invite Users to your Organization’s RaterPRO Account’ in the RaterPRO Desktop User Guide for guidance on how to do so. Once invited, those users will receive an invitation email and should follow the same steps below to get started.
